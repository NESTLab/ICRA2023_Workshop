Call for Papers

The workshop will include a call for papers. The papers will be presented in two poster sessions, and selected papers will also be presented as highlighted contributions.

Topics of Interest

  • Applications of multi-robot systems and swarms
  • Field deployment of multi-robot systems and swarms
  • Coordination algorithms for multi-robot systems and swarms
  • Machine learning for multi-robot systems and swarms
  • Programming and software engineering for multi-robot systems and swarms
  • Fault tolerance in multi-robot systems and swarms
